# Introducing Cedar Craft - videos for developers learning Cedar

Cedar Craft is a new video series that gets you started working with Cedar. The series has been created by [Zachary Tatlock](https://ztatlock.net/), associate professor from the University of Washington and visiting scholar at AWS, to share his experiences and lessons learned. Zachary has posted seven videos so far,


* [Setting up Cedar in Python with cedarpy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CUvtp3noY50)
* [Basic Introduction to Cedar Entity Attributes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UYj5SBOiUCk)
* [Enriching Cedar Policies and the Need for Validation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eBUerHynfYU)
* [Validating Cedar Policies at the Command Line](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HN6r4tbh0nc)
* [Cedar VSCode Extension: Easy Policy Validation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=n95_HMPPn1c)
* [Cedar Agent: Use Cedar Everywhere!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OX3rNuA5zWU)
* [Cedar Policies: Default Deny and Avoiding Evaluation Errors with Validation](https://youtu.be/aHFCjqONbxI?si=DKuxRjnLcfmbDVI3)


Visit the channel at the link below and don’t forget to subscribe to catch new content.
